Privacy Policy — Shorts Publisher

Last updated: 2026-07-17

Shorts Publisher ("the app") is a private, in-house tool used by the owner to publish short videos, created by the owner, to the owner's own social media accounts (currently TikTok and Instagram) through each platform's official API. The app is not offered to the public and has no users other than the owner.

Data we access

  • An OAuth access token and refresh token, granted by the account owner, used solely to publish videos on the owner's own connected accounts.
  • Basic account information returned by the platform (e.g. account id, display name) to confirm the connected account.

How we use it

The tokens are used only to upload and publish video content that the owner has created, to the owner's own connected accounts. We do not use the data for any other purpose.

Storage & sharing

  • Tokens are stored locally on the owner's machine only. They are never transmitted to any third party.
  • We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data of any third party. The app has no users other than the owner.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Retention & deletion

Tokens can be revoked at any time by the owner from the relevant platform's account settings, and the local token file can be deleted at any time, which fully disconnects the app from that platform. See our Data Deletion Instructions for details.

Contact

For any question about this policy, contact the app owner at the email associated with the relevant platform's developer account.
