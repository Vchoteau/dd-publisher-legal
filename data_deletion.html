Data Deletion Instructions — Shorts Publisher

Last updated: 2026-07-18

Shorts Publisher ("the app") is a private, in-house tool with no users other than its owner. It does not collect or store any data belonging to third parties.

What is stored

The only data retained by the app are OAuth access/refresh tokens for the owner's own connected accounts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube), stored locally on the owner's machine.

How to delete it

The owner can delete all locally stored data at any time by removing the local token files. Revoking the app's access from within each platform's own account settings (e.g. TikTok > Settings > Manage apps, or Instagram/Facebook > Settings > Apps and websites) immediately and permanently invalidates the corresponding tokens on the platform's side.

Third-party data

Since the app has no end users other than its owner and does not process data belonging to any other individual, there is no third-party data to delete.

Contact

For any data deletion request or question, contact the app owner at the email associated with the relevant platform's developer account.
